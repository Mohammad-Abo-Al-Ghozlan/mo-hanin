Built for critical power environments

Electrical engineers who can design a panel, program its controller, commission the system, and maintain it under pressure are rare. Mohamad El Hanin operates across the full lifecycle — from schematic design and assembly through to fault diagnosis and long-term maintenance.

At Specialized Power System Supplies in Abu Dhabi, Mohamad has been responsible for delivering LV switchgear panels — including ATS/AMF, generator control, and fuel systems — for clients where power availability is non-negotiable. Projects for Etisalat and the UAE Ministry of Defense demand that standard.

He brings programming depth across Schneider PLCs, Deep Sea generator controllers, and Delta PLCs, alongside hands-on capability in AutoCAD, SolidWorks, MATLAB, and simulation tools.
